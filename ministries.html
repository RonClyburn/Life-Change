CHILDREN'S CHURCH

Through music, stories, crafts, snacks and games our teachers bring the Word alive. Our desire is to encourage each child to get to know the God of the bible for themselves.

Church classes are divided into two age groups. The first is for 4 - 6 years. The other is for 7 years - 5th grade. We meet in the ARK during regular weekly service times.

YOUTH GROUP

Life Change Church Youth Group exists to share the love of God with young people.

Combined Youth - Middle & High School Meets Sundays from 6:00 - 7:30pm in the church building.

It is an excellent time of teaching, snacks, games, and fun designed to bring the Youth of today closer to the God that loves them.

WORSHIP

Worship is our whole-life response to the revelation of who God is and what He does.

We spend time in corporate worship at Life Change Church because we believe that lasting change in our lives comes from revelation.

When we truly consider the greatness of God and His love for us we cannot help but be changed.

NURSERY

We have a dedicated and enthusiastic team of helpers that come each Sunday looking forward to spending time with your child from newborn to age 3.

Our desire is that you as parents can be confident your child is being well cared for while you are able to hear the Word of God in service.

There are many activities to help your child enjoy their time in the nursery such as coloring, books, blocks and other age appropriate toys.
